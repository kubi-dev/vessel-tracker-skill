# Persian Gulf Vessels — Weekly Tracking Report (Mar 3–10, 2026)

## Context

Since February 28, 2026, the Strait of Hormuz has been effectively closed following joint US-Israeli military strikes on Iran (Operation Epic Fury). Iran's IRGC issued warnings prohibiting vessel passage. As of March 9, traffic through the strait dropped to near-zero (two outbound Iranian-flagged vessels, no inbound crossings). Around 200 compliant tankers are stranded inside the Persian Gulf, including 60 VLCCs (~8% of global compliant VLCC fleet). Over 1,650 vessels experienced GPS/AIS interference on March 7 alone, a 55% weekly increase.
