## New Incidents Check (Mar 8-10, 2026)

**No new vessel attacks have been confirmed since Mar 7.**

Key developments Mar 8-10:
- **Mar 8:** Strait of Hormuz crossings fell to just 2 vessels (both Iranian-flagged, outbound only). Zero inbound commercial traffic.
- **Mar 9:** One tanker conducted a dark transit (AIS disabled ~5 days) — loaded ~1M barrels from Saudi Juaymah Terminal (signal loss Mar 4, resumption ~Mar 9). Identity unconfirmed but possibly Dynacom-operated.
- **Mar 9:** Iran foreign ministry warned oil tankers transiting Strait of Hormuz to be "very careful."
- **Mar 10:** Shadow fleet/Iranian-flagged vessels dominate the few crossings. Iran ramping up bypass loadings.
- **GPS Jamming:** 1,650+ vessels affected on Mar 7 (55% increase from prior week).
- **Insurance:** War-risk coverage withdrawn for much of the Gulf, creating de facto closure for commercial market.
