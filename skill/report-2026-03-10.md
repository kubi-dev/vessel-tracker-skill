## Geopolitical Context

On February 28, 2026, US and Israel launched coordinated strikes on Iran ("Operation Epic Fury"). Iran's IRGC declared the Strait of Hormuz closed. Key facts:

- Transit collapsed ~92% (from 153 to ~13 vessels/day); by Mar 8, only 2 outbound Iranian-flagged vessels crossed — zero inbound
- ~200 compliant tankers stranded in the Persian Gulf, including ~60 VLCCs (~8% of global compliant VLCC fleet)
- 13 commercial vessels attacked by Iranian drones/missiles/drone boats (up from 11 in initial reports)
- At least 7 crew members killed across multiple incidents
- No new attacks since Mar 7 — traffic at near-zero, de facto blockade in effect
- Widespread GPS jamming/spoofing (1,650+ vessels affected on Mar 7 alone, 55% weekly increase, 44 signal injection zones)
- Insurance companies dropped war risk coverage for the region
- VLCC charter rates hit all-time highs ($770,000/day for Kalamos by Bharat Petroleum)
